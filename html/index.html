About Us
Artisan Cake Design by Tamar Lev

Tamar picked up her baking skills as a child in her mother’s kitchen.
Tamar uses edible paint in a large array of colors for her original illustrations.
Her inspired designs come from both her own vision and discussions with each client.
Tamar’s novelty cakes are recognizable as some of the best in the business.
They range from a cake painted in an animal print, to an Adidas sneaker and even a replica of The American White House.
If you can imagine it, Tamar can create it.
